Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jan 2026

We respect your privacy and believe in being transparent about how data is used.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What Data We Collect

If you choose to opt in, we collect anonymous gameplay analytics, which may include:

  • Gameplay events (e.g. levels completed, cards used, deaths, wins/losses)
  • Performance data (e.g. crashes, errors, load times)
  • Game version, platform, and basic device information
  • General play patterns used for balancing and testing

Why We Collect This Data

We collect analytics data solely to:

  • Improve game balance and design
  • Identify bugs and crashes
  • Support playtesting and development decisions
  • Maintain the long-term health of the game and its community

Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, our legal basis for collecting this data is:

Your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a))

Analytics data is only collected if you actively opt in.

Data Retention

We keep analytics data only as long as it benefits the game and its community, including for development, balancing, and stability purposes.

Data may be deleted or aggregated in an anonymized way when it is no longer useful.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you choose to opt in, we collect anonymous gameplay analytics, which may include:

  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request access to collected data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

You can withdraw consent at any time via the in-game settings.

Children’s Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Analytics data is anonymous and used only for game development purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated in-game or on our website.

How to Contact Us

For inquiries or press requests, reach us at hello@polykitegames.com.
