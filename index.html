Polykite Games

Polykite Games is an Icelandic independent games studio dedicated to creating playful worlds with emotional depth and technical craft. We explore games as spaces for wonder, storytelling, and joy.
Lead by 2 veterans, each with over a decade of experience in AAA. The team is super excited about our debut title!

Our Philosophy

We believe that small teams can make big experiences. Our approach combines experimentation with a strong artistic vision, focusing on meaning as much as mechanics.

Bee Space

Our upcoming title, Bee Space, invites players to explore a world abandoned by people through the eyes of the bees themselves. Bee Space is a cozy, roguelike, deckbuilder where you guide curious Space Bees as they regrow tiny islands on Earth.

A Cozy Deckbuilder

Partner with different bees on your campaign to regrow Earth. Every new Bee partner brings a new story as well as new mechanics. Bee Space has been designed to be accessible while offering strategic depth. The game has constructiveness at its core and contains no violence.

Charms and Artifacts

Discover synergies between human charms and cards. Uncover strange human artifacts that change the game rules in fun ways on each island!

Handcrafted Visuals

Bee Space takes visual inspiration from the space age. With stylized visuals and soothing music the bees will charm your socks off.
